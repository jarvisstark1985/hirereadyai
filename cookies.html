1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit a website. They help websites recognize you on return visits and remember your preferences.

Cookies typically contain:

  • • A unique identifier
  • • The domain that set the cookie
  • • An expiration date
  • • Information about your visit or preferences

Cookies are not harmful and do not contain malware. They are used for legitimate business and user experience purposes.

2. Why We Use Cookies

Resume SuperHero uses cookies to:

  • • Authenticate Users: Keep you logged in to your account securely
  • • Remember Preferences: Store your language, theme, and display preferences
  • • Analyze Usage: Understand how you use our platform to improve features
  • • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and abuse
  • • Personalization: Customize content and recommendations based on your interests
  • • Marketing: Track effectiveness of our marketing campaigns
  • • Session Management: Maintain your session across multiple pages

6. How to Manage Cookies

Browser Controls

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through settings:

  • • Chrome: Menu > Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • • Firefox: Menu > Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • • Edge: Settings > Privacy, search and services > Clear data

Cookie Preferences

You can manage which types of cookies we use by adjusting your preferences. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect Service functionality.

Do Not Track

If your browser supports "Do Not Track," we will respect that signal for analytics cookies, though essential cookies cannot be disabled.

Third-Party Opt-Out

You can opt-out of third-party tracking:

  • • Google Analytics Opt-Out
  • • Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out
  • • Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out

Note: Disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of our Service, including account login and personalization.

7. Cookie Consent

When you first visit Resume SuperHero, you will see a cookie consent banner allowing you to:

  • • Accept All: Allow all cookies, including analytics and marketing
  • • Reject Non-Essential: Accept only essential cookies required for the Service
  • • Customize: Choose which types of cookies to allow

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking "Cookie Settings" in the website footer. Your consent is stored for 12 months.

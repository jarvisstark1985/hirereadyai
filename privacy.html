1. Overview

Resume SuperHero ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our career coaching platform, including all associated features and services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service. Your continued use of our Service following the posting of revised Privacy Policy means that you accept and agree to the changes.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

  • • Account Registration: Name, email address, phone number, password, and profile information
  • • Resume Data: Your resume content, work history, education, skills, and certifications
  • • Career Information: Career goals, preferred industries, job titles, salary expectations, and location preferences
  • • Job Applications: Records of jobs you've applied to, application dates, and employer responses
  • • LinkedIn Data: If you connect your LinkedIn profile, we collect your profile information with your permission
  • • Interview Recordings: Audio and video recordings of practice interviews you conduct with our platform
  • • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely by Stripe)
  • • Communication Data: Messages, feedback, and support requests you send us

Information Automatically Collected

  • • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address
  • • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform, click patterns, search queries
  • • Cookies & Tracking: Persistent identifiers, session cookies, analytics tracking
  • • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (not precise geolocation)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • • Service Delivery: Providing, maintaining, and improving our AI-powered resume building, interview coaching, and career roadmap features
  • • AI Processing: Training and operating our machine learning models to generate personalized resume suggestions, job matches, and interview feedback
  • • Personalization: Customizing your experience based on your career goals and preferences
  • • Billing & Payment: Processing subscriptions, invoicing, and payment transactions
  • • Communication: Sending service updates, feature announcements, and customer support responses
  • • Analytics: Understanding user behavior to improve platform functionality and user experience
  • • Security: Detecting, preventing, and addressing fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • • Legal Compliance: Fulfilling legal obligations and enforcing our Terms of Service
  • • Marketing: Sending promotional content (with your opt-in consent) about new features and special offers

4. Third-Party Services

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business:

Stripe (Payment Processing)

We share payment information with Stripe to process your subscription payments. Stripe is PCI DSS compliant and encrypts your payment data. See Stripe's privacy policy.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to track website traffic and user behavior. Google collects data through cookies and may share limited information with third parties. See Google's privacy policy.

Content Delivery Network (CDN)

We use CDN providers to deliver website assets and improve performance. These providers may log your IP address and browser information.

OpenAI / AI Service Providers

To provide AI-powered features, we may process your resume and career data through third-party AI services. Your data is pseudonymized and used only to improve your experience.

We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. We only share information necessary to provide our Service.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information as follows:

  • • Active Accounts: While your account is active, we retain all your information to provide the Service
  • • After Cancellation: After you cancel your subscription, we retain your data for 30 days. You may request immediate deletion during this period
  • • Legal Requirements: We may retain data longer if required by law, regulation, or legitimate business purposes (e.g., tax records for 7 years)
  • • Aggregated Data: We indefinitely retain anonymized and aggregated data that cannot identify you

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at jarvis.stark1985@gmail.com.

7. CCPA Compliance (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • • Right to Know: You can request what personal information we collect, use, and share
  • • Right to Delete: You can request deletion of personal information (with limited exceptions)
  • • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt-out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information
  • • Right to Correct: You can request correction of inaccurate information
  • • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights

To submit a CCPA request, email jarvis.stark1985@gmail.com with "CCPA Request" in the subject line. We will respond within 45 days.

8. GDPR Compliance (EU Residents)

If you are located in the European Union, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • • Legal Basis: We process your data based on your consent, contract performance, and legitimate business interests
  • • Right to Access: You can request access to your personal data
  • • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate data
  • • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion under certain circumstances
  • • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request limitation of how we process your data
  • • Right to Data Portability: You can export your data in a portable format
  • • Right to Object: You can object to certain processing activities

To exercise GDPR rights, email jarvis.stark1985@gmail.com with "GDPR Request" in the subject line. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Resume SuperHero is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete such information and terminate the child's account.

For users ages 13-18, we provide the same privacy protections as for adults. If you are under 18, please ask your parent or guardian before using our Service.
